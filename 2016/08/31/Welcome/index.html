Welcome to my blog

Aug 31, 2016 | sitelog | Hits

I used wordpress and bo-blog before and I found it’s hard to just focus on writing. So I choose hexo to generate the static site with the markdown source file and deploy it on the github. The tutorial to setup the hexo can be found everywhere on the Internet so that it’s easy to start and I can quickly write my first post like this one.

I’ve planned to build this blog for a long time, in order to record my experiences and thoughts. I stepped in the GPU field two years ago with a job of driver development. It’s very hard for a novice to learn the technology of GPU driver because it refers to too much knowledge such as computer graphics concepts, graphics API(DirectX, OpenGL), WDDM and even hardware flows. I suffered from learning these and finally strung them together to have a clear concept of the GPU. But the course is very difficult and I think it’s very significant to write the learning experience down. And now, I’m working on GPU cmodel, which is more difficult than driver I think. So it’s necessary to take the studying notes, working summary and some thoughts about the technology.

I will try my best to update this blog to record and share my learning experiences.

Sorry for my Chinglish, but it’s why I choose English for writing.
